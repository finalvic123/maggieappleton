## A distinctive speedometer illustration for the Google Chrome team.

<br />
<br />

<BasicImage src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dg3gyk0gu/image/upload/c_scale,w_80/v1589143431/maggieappleton.com/google-speedometer/8PkUL_g8f1DeQAAAAASUVORK5CYII.png" />

</Center>


<TwoParagraph>

<p>Addy Osmani from the Google Chrome team got in touch when they needed a new design for their Speedometer tool. The tool tests how fast a web app responds, given a pile of simulated user interactions like mouse movemenets, clicks, and keyboard input. </p>

<p>The team wanted a distinctive illustrated badge of a speedometer to use across the website and branding.
Given my love of finely-rendered mechanical details, the project was a great fit.</p>

</TwoParagraph>
